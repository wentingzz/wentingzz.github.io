Audio Summary

AudioSummary empowers users to unlock the essence of spoken content effortlessly. Transforming lengthy audio files into concise, informative summaries, it saves time and enhances productivity. With AudioSummary, dive deeper into the world of audio content, uncovering key insights with ease. See Demo

Key Features

Speech-to-Text

Utilizes the Hugging Face Transformers library for speech recognition, converting audio content from uploaded files or URLs into text

Prompt Template

Chains together language models (LLAMA-2 model) with prompt templates to facilitate the summarization process, allowing for more coherent and accurate summaries.

LLM-Powered Summarization

Utilizes the LLAMA-2 model for advanced language model-based summarization.

Audio File Management

Manages audio files for processing, including downloading and deleting temporary files.
