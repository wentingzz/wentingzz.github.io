NutriPlan

NutriPlan is designed to address the critical need for improved nutrition and health habits. Research shows that up to 80% of heart disease, stroke, and type 2 diabetes, as well as up to 40% of cancer cases, could be prevented by adopting healthier eating habits, engaging in regular physical activity, and quitting tobacco use. NutriPlan aims to empower users to make these positive lifestyle changes by providing a convenient and effective tool for meal planning and nutrition tracking. See Demo

Key Features

Recipe Recommendations

Provides users with healthy recipe suggestions to inspire and diversify their meal choices while aligning with their dietary preferences and restrictions.

Progress Tracking

Enables users to monitor their progress towards their fitness & nutrition goals, including weight management and overall health improvement.

User Profile & Preferences

Generates customized meal plans based on individual health goals, nutritional needs, and allergen restrictions.

Food Database

Provides informed calorie and nutrition information to make educated meal choices.
